In the interest of further exploring form elements, I also found a page about on W3Schools. On there, I found another cool element called a Fieldset. This element is used simply as way to visually group together multiple related elements. I also found on there how to create a standard button element. Overall, This element category appears extremely useful and practical for many applications.
